## Epic Children: Sex Ratio of Children of Key Characters in Epics

[Data](epic_children.csv) on the number and sex of children of key characters in epics.

In Ramayan and Mahabharat, across 117 characters, the median number of children is 2 and the average is ~16. The sex ratio is nearly 25 and if you take out people with 50 or more kids, it drops to ~5.
